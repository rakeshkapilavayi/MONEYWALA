Our Inspiration

One day, I witnessed a friend struggling with a money issue. He needed cash to buy food and essentials but only had money in his UPI wallet. At the same time, another person nearby needed UPI funds but only had cash. Both searched their locality for help, but no one could assist. After hours of struggle, they finally met, exchanged their money, and resolved their issues. This experience highlighted the challenges people face in urgent situations when they can’t find the right mode of payment. Inspired by their struggle, we created Moneywala—a platform that connects people to exchange UPI and cash seamlessly, ensuring no one faces such difficulties again.
